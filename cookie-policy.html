What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your computer, tablet or mobile device when you visit a website. They help websites remember information about your visit and improve your browsing experience.

Cookies Used By This Website

At present, Santulan Therapy Clinic does not use advertising cookies, profiling cookies or marketing cookies. Only essential browser functionality may be used where required for normal website operation.

Third-Party Services

This website may provide links to third-party services including:

These services may use cookies according to their own privacy and cookie policies. Please review their respective policies before using those services.

Managing Cookies

Most modern web browsers allow you to:

  • View stored cookies
  • Delete cookies
  • Block specific cookies
  • Block all cookies
  • Receive notifications before cookies are stored

Please note that disabling certain cookies may affect the functionality of some websites.

Future Changes

If analytics tools, appointment systems or other services requiring cookies are added in the future, this Cookie Policy will be updated accordingly.
