Understanding Anxiety

What is Anxiety & Stress?

Feeling anxious or stressed from time to time is a natural part of life. However, when these feelings become frequent, intense or begin affecting your work, relationships, sleep or overall wellbeing, professional counselling can help. Therapy provides a safe and supportive space to understand your emotions, identify the underlying causes and develop healthier ways to cope with life's challenges.

Signs & Symptoms

Common Signs of Anxiety & Stress

Every person experiences anxiety differently. If these symptoms persist or begin interfering with your daily life, seeking support can make a meaningful difference.

Excessive Worrying

Constant worrying about everyday situations, even when there is little reason for concern.

Difficulty Sleeping

Trouble falling asleep, waking frequently or feeling tired despite adequate rest.

Feeling Overwhelmed

Everyday responsibilities may begin to feel difficult to manage because of persistent stress.

Difficulty Concentrating

Stress and anxiety can affect focus, memory and decision-making in daily life.

Irritability

Feeling easily frustrated, emotionally reactive or experiencing mood changes more often.

Physical Symptoms

Headaches, muscle tension, fatigue, rapid heartbeat or digestive discomfort may sometimes accompany prolonged stress.

Frequently Asked Questions

Questions People Often Ask

When should I seek counselling for anxiety?

If anxiety or stress is affecting your daily routine, relationships, work, studies or overall wellbeing, professional counselling can help you better understand your emotions and develop healthy coping strategies.

Are counselling sessions confidential?

Yes. Every counselling session is conducted in a confidential, respectful and supportive environment while adhering to applicable ethical and legal responsibilities.

How many counselling sessions will I need?

The number of sessions varies from person to person depending on individual concerns, goals and progress. This can be discussed during your initial consultation.

Can stress affect physical health?

Yes. Long-term stress may contribute to headaches, muscle tension, sleep difficulties, digestive discomfort and fatigue. Counselling can help you develop healthier ways to manage stress.

Do you provide counselling in Hindi?

Yes. Counselling sessions are available in both English and Hindi to ensure clients can communicate comfortably.
