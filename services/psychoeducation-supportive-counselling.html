Understanding Mental Wellbeing

What is Psychoeducation & Supportive Counselling?

Psychoeducation helps individuals better understand their thoughts, emotions and behaviours through evidence-based mental health education, while supportive counselling provides compassionate guidance during life's challenges. Together, they empower individuals to make informed decisions, strengthen emotional resilience and develop practical coping strategies that support long-term mental wellbeing.

Frequently Asked Questions

Questions People Often Ask

What is psychoeducation?

Psychoeducation is a therapeutic approach that helps individuals understand mental health, emotions, behaviours and coping strategies. It empowers people with practical knowledge to better manage everyday challenges.

How is supportive counselling different from therapy?

Supportive counselling focuses on providing emotional support, guidance and practical coping strategies. It helps individuals navigate current life challenges while promoting resilience, confidence and emotional wellbeing.

Who can benefit from psychoeducation?

Anyone can benefit, including students, parents, working professionals, young adults and individuals who want to improve emotional awareness, develop healthy coping skills and better understand their mental wellbeing.

Will I learn practical coping techniques?

Yes. Sessions include evidence-based coping strategies, stress management techniques, emotional regulation skills and practical tools that can be applied in everyday life.

Are sessions available in English and Hindi?

Yes. Sessions are available in both English and Hindi so clients can communicate comfortably in the language they prefer.
